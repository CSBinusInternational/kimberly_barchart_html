DISTRIBUTION OF EMBARKMENT LOCATIONS ON THE TITANIC

There were many passengers on the Titanic that embarked the ship from 3 different places. The bar chart displays the distribution of the passengers from each place. S stands for Southampton, C for Cherbourg and Q for Queenstown. Majority came from Southampton at roughly 69.93%. 20.66% came from Cherbourg and the remaining 9.41% came from Queenstown.
